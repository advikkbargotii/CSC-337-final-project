Getting Started

Creating Your Account

BudgetMasters uses a simple username-based login system. To get started:

  1. Visit the login page
  2. Enter your desired username
  3. Click "Login/Register"
  4. If the username exists, you'll log in; if not, a new account will be created

Managing Budgets

Creating a Budget

To create a new budget:

  1. Click "Create New Budget" on the dashboard
  2. Enter the budget name
  3. Set the total budget limit
  4. Add categories and their individual limits
  5. Click "Create Budget" to save

Tracking Expenses

Adding Expenses

To log a new expense:

  1. Go to the Expenses page or click "Add Expense" on a budget card
  2. Select the budget and category
  3. Enter the amount and description
  4. Select the date
  5. Click "Add Expense" to save

Using Charts

Understanding Your Data

The Charts page provides visual insights into your spending:

  • Pie Chart: Shows spending distribution across categories
  • Bar Chart: Displays monthly spending trends
  • Line Chart: Tracks daily spending patterns
  • Use filters to analyze specific time periods or budgets

Leaderboard

Understanding Rankings

The leaderboard ranks users based on their savings percentage:

  • Rankings update automatically as you manage your budgets
  • Higher savings percentages lead to better rankings
  • Filter rankings by different time periods
  • View your personal stats at the top of the page
